Series 65 Exam: How Many Questions Can You Miss?

You can miss about 38 of the 130 scored questions on the Series 65 and still pass. Passing score: 71% — roughly 92 correct answers.

Why "about": FINRA (exam delivery/enrollment; NASAA is the exam owner — file never uses the word "administrator") uses scaled scoring, and 10 of the 140 questions are unscored pretest items you can't identify. The numbers here are the published specs — treat the margin as a close approximation, not a guarantee.
